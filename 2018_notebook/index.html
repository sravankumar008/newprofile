intresting facts about space

  • Time Dilation: According to Einstein's theory of relativity, time moves slower in strong gravitational fields. This means that astronauts on the International Space Station age just slightly slower than those on Earth due to the station's speed and distance from Earth's gravitational pull.
  • The Great Red Spot: Jupiter's iconic Great Red Spot is a colossal storm that has been raging for at least 400 years. It's so massive that three Earths could fit inside it.
  • Voyager 1: Launched in 1977, Voyager 1 is the farthest man-made object from Earth. It has ventured beyond our solar system and continues its journey into interstellar space, carrying a golden record with greetings, music, and sounds from Earth.
